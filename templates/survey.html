SURVEY FORM FOR EMPLOYEE EMOTION HEALTH MONITORING

Question 1: How satisfied are you with your job overall?

Question 2: Do you feel that your contributions to the company are valued?

Question 3: How satisfied are you with your work-life balance?

Question 4: Do you feel that you have opportunities for growth and advancement within the company?

Question 5: How satisfied are you with your current workload?

Question 6: How would you rate the level of support you receive from your manager?

Question 7: How often do you receive feedback on your work?

Question 8: How satisfied are you with your compensation package?

Question 9: How comfortable do you feel with the level of communication within the company?

Question 10: How well do you feel your ideas are heard and valued by your supervisor?

Question 11: How would you rate the level of support you receive from your colleagues?

Question 12: How satisfied are you with the level of communication from management regarding company updates and changes?

Question 13: How would you rate the company's commitment to employee development and training?

Question 14: How would you rate the level of collaboration among teams?

Question 15: How would you rate the level of transparency within the company?

Question 16: How would you rate the company's commitment to diversity and inclusion?
